# Boundary data licence and attribution

The derived Odisha district GeoJSON in this directory is produced from the
DataMeet India community **India District Map**, Census 2011 edition, pinned to
commit `b3fbbde595310b397a55d718e0958ce249a4fa1f`.

Source: <https://github.com/datameet/maps/tree/b3fbbde595310b397a55d718e0958ce249a4fa1f/Districts/Census_2011>

The dataset-specific `Districts/README.md` releases the district dataset under
the [Creative Commons Attribution 2.5 India licence](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.5/in/).

Attribution: **India District Map by the DataMeet India community, CC BY 2.5
India.** The Odisha Public Health Intelligence Hub transformed the source by
filtering Odisha, adding stable internal identifiers and reconciling display
names. No endorsement by DataMeet or the Census of India is implied.

This is a Census 2011 community boundary used for the competition evidence and
coverage map. It is not represented as the current authoritative State boundary
for operational allocation.
